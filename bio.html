Bio

Curious and dedicated.

Origin

Born in Hialeah, FL, USA, I started off gifted. My parents came from Argentina seeking a better life. It was just my mom, dad and I, supported by my grandmother at times. My father would always be out trying to find anything and everything for the family, while my mother took care of me.

I admit, I was a troublesome child, but that did not stop me from being smart, creative, and a good friend.

School

My parents would always say I'm special. Despite all the trouble I caused, I did well in elementary school. I had taken part in spelling bees, earned awards and medals, took gifted classes, created art, and was always attracted to the math and science—I have a short book about myself with atoms and molecules drawn on the cover, inspired by Jimmy Neutron.

Unfortunately, I got side-tracked during middle and high school. My own nature backfired and interfered with my everyday life, holding me back from my max potential. Even today, I'm not close to that; however, progress is progress. I am still learning and improving myself, one day at a time.

Family

I currently live with a family of 5, including me: mom, dad, sister (middle), and brother (youngest). The rest of my family is back at the hometown of Argentina, which I had visited once. We have a small circle of family friends and we see them numerous times a month despite our busy schedule.

Interests

I enjoy listening to music, playing video games, and learning new things. I'm particularly interested in science and technology, especially when it comes to understanding how things work and how they can be improved. There is only one thing I do not like: microwave.
